Welcome!

Smog, traffic, filth, and crowds.
Industialized synthetic foods, a dearth of trees, a general sense of stress, and a lack of community.
Isolation in the mob, time passing quickly, quietly. No recolection of why or how.
Our life in the city (we tried several: New York, San Fransisco, Chicago) left us sick, depressed, unfulfilled and longing.
Then we came here. {{site.land_name}}.
Here we found something simple, something real, something wholesome and healing.
It is something that doesn't exist yet, but it's ours if we make it.
This is our homestead. This is our journey. This is {{site.land_name}}.
